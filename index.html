DESI Spectra Analysis
Explore the complexities of DESI spectra, where coefficients derived from various reduction methods, combined with two machine learning regression models, predict physical characteristics of DESI galaxies. Vital for future DESI spectral analysis, this project provides a robust methodology for swift and accurate estimation of galactic properties.

Key Questions:

  • What is the optimal number of PCA coefficients for accurate predictions?
  • Comparing PCA and UMAP coefficients, which is more effective in predicting parameters?
  • Which combination of coefficients and machine learning models yields the most precise predictions?
